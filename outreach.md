**Surveys**

As part of my PhD research, I released an online survey for Northern Beaches residents which was promoted in the **Manly Daily** newspaper and local magazine **Pittwater Life**. We asked residents whether they encounter ticks in their yard and how often, what wildlife they see in their yard and how often, and we asked about particular yard traits that we predicted would influence tick or host presence in yards.
